# GV2 Character Lore — The Garuda Trials

## World Setting

Two civilizations share the skies of Akasya. The **Sarathi** — descendants of mythological factions bound by ancient Astra powers and Thara Resonance — pilot the sacred Vimana. The **Atom Riders** — citizens of modern nations wielding Nuclear-class weapons systems — fly the Spaceship. Both compete in **The Garuda Trials**, an aerial combat tournament held above the contested Akasa Raajyas, where dominance of the sky determines the fate of kingdoms and countries alike.

Every pilot has a reason to fight. None of them trust each other.

---

## SARATHI PILOTS (B_Vimana)

*Kingdoms of the Akasa Raajyas — Astra-powered warriors from the nine ancient kingdoms.*

---

### 1. AARYAVEER — Heir of Nandana

**Rarity:** Epic | **Role:** Balanced Striker
**Kingdom:** Nandana | **Faction:** Devas
**Powers:** Supreme Astra Powers, Dual Terrain Mastery, Normal Thara Resonance

**Backstory:**
Crown prince of Nandana, the highest kingdom of the Devas. His father ruled unchallenged until Roudra — his most trusted general — turned the Asura forces of Daitya Giri against him. The king survived but the throne fractured, and the other kingdoms sensed weakness. Aaryaveer enters the Garuda Trials to reunify Nandana's shattered alliances and prove the Devas still hold supremacy over the Akasa Raajyas. He wields Supreme Astra — the highest tier of divine weaponry — but his Thara Resonance is only Normal, meaning he burns through power fast and must fight smart to outlast opponents with deeper reserves.

**Strengths:**
- Versatile across all combat situations — no critical gap in his abilities
- Supreme Astra grants access to the most powerful weapon class among Sarathi
- Natural leader; Dual Terrain Mastery lets him fight effectively in any arena

**Weaknesses:**
- Normal Thara Resonance — burns power faster than Yakshas or Manavas pilots
- Emotionally compromised by Roudra's betrayal; hesitates against former allies
- Spreads his focus too thin trying to master everything rather than specializing

**Rivals:** Roudra (Asura warlord who broke his kingdom), Kaevik (QiNEXi arms dealer suspected of supplying Roudra's weapons tech)

---

### 2. ISHVAYA — Sentinel of Ratna Shila

**Rarity:** Rare | **Role:** Precision / Control
**Kingdom:** Ratna Shila | **Faction:** Yakshas
**Powers:** Celestial Astra Powers, Multi Terrain Mastery, Advance Thara Resonance

**Backstory:**
Sworn guardian of Ratna Shila — the jeweled fortress-kingdom of the Yakshas, keepers of sacred treasures hidden since the age of the Vimana builders. Ishvaya was raised in the Yaksha warrior tradition: measure twice, strike once, waste nothing. Her Celestial Astra may not match the raw power of Supreme-class weapons, but her Advance Thara Resonance means she can sustain combat far longer than most, wearing opponents down with patient precision. She never wanted the Garuda Trials. She was forced in when Vyanika's Garudaja raiders began striking Ratna Shila's outer vaults, and when Nysera — an Atom Rider intelligence broker from Inkara — sold Ratna Shila's defensive layouts to the highest bidder.

**Strengths:**
- Unmatched precision at range; every shot is calculated
- Advance Thara Resonance gives her deep energy reserves for sustained fights
- Multi Terrain Mastery makes her effective across varied battlefields

**Weaknesses:**
- Rigid Yaksha honor codes make her predictable to those who study her
- Celestial Astra lacks the raw burst of Supreme-class weapons
- Close-range chaos exposes her reliance on spacing and control

**Rivals:** Vyanika (Garudaja raider hitting her kingdom's vaults), Nysera (INTARi data-thief who sold Ratna Shila's secrets)

---

### 3. VYANIKA — Windrider of Garuda Vana

**Rarity:** Epic | **Role:** Mobility / Evasive
**Kingdom:** Garuda Vana | **Faction:** Garudajas
**Powers:** Supreme Astra Powers, Dual Terrain Mastery, Normal Thara Resonance

**Backstory:**
Born in Garuda Vana — the sky-kingdom of the Garudajas, perched on floating peaks above perpetual storms. Life among the Garudajas means constant motion; their territory shifts with the winds, and only the fastest survive. Garuda Vana is dying — its ancient Vimana stabilizers are failing, and the kingdom needs resources the lowland realms refuse to share. Vyanika leads raids on Ratna Shila's outer vaults and Nandana's supply caravans. She knows Ishvaya has sworn to stop her. She knows Aaryaveer wants to annex Garuda Vana's flight technology. Roudra in Daitya Giri simply wants the Garudajas crushed. Vyanika fights because for the Garudajas, standing still means falling.

**Strengths:**
- Fastest pilot in the Akasa Raajyas; untouchable when she has space to move
- Supreme Astra combined with Dual Terrain Mastery — lethal in the right hands
- Controls the tempo of any engagement; she decides when and where fights happen

**Weaknesses:**
- Normal Thara Resonance — she can't sustain long engagements; must end fights fast
- Paper-thin offense despite Supreme Astra class; speed comes at the cost of firepower
- The survival of Garuda Vana rests on her shoulders — the pressure fractures her focus

**Rivals:** Ishvaya (defends the vaults she raids), Roudra (wants Garuda Vana destroyed), Aaryaveer (Nandana wants her flight tech)

---

### 4. ROUDRA — Warlord of Daitya Giri

**Rarity:** Legendary | **Role:** Heavy Burst
**Kingdom:** Daitya Giri | **Faction:** Asuras
**Powers:** Supreme Astra Powers, Dual Terrain Mastery, Normal Thara Resonance

**Backstory:**
Once the most trusted general serving Nandana's Deva throne, Roudra served Aaryaveer's father for twenty years before discovering the truth: Nandana's golden supremacy was built on Daitya Giri's suffering. The Asura forges fed the Deva empire, Asura miners died in the volcanic depths, and the Deva king called it divine order. Roudra didn't betray Nandana — he liberated Daitya Giri. He wears the Om symbol on his chest plate as a reminder that divine authority means nothing without justice. Now he commands the volcanic forges, building the most devastating Astra weapons the Akasa Raajyas have ever seen. In the Garuda Trials, nothing survives a direct hit from Roudra. The question is whether you can avoid him long enough.

**Strengths:**
- Highest burst damage in the entire roster — Supreme Astra channeled through Asura forge-weapons
- Armor forged from Daitya Giri volcanic ore; nearly indestructible head-on
- Decades of battlefield experience — reads formations, not individuals

**Weaknesses:**
- Slowest pilot in the Trials; his weapons platform sacrifices all agility for firepower
- Normal Thara Resonance — devastating but unsustainable in prolonged fights
- Old injuries from the Forge Wars slow his reflexes; relies on reading attacks before they come

**Rivals:** Aaryaveer (the prince wants vengeance), Zorvan (NEO-TERRAn who deserted his ranks), Vyanika (Garudaja raiders threatening his supply lines)

## ATOM RIDER PILOTS (A_Spaceship)

*Earth Countries in Akasya — Nuclear-powered combatants from the nine modern nations.*

---

### 5. ZORVAN — Deserter of Aurkana

**Rarity:** Rare | **Role:** Missile Hunter
**Country:** Aurkana | **Culture:** NEO-TERRANs (North American inspired)
**Powers:** Antimatter Class Nuclear P, Dual Terrain Mastery, Normal Thara Resonance

**Backstory:**
Zorvan served as a missile systems officer in Aurkana's NEO-TERRAn military — the most technologically advanced armed force among the Earth Countries. He was assigned as a liaison to Roudra's Asura liberation campaign, part of Aurkana's covert support for Daitya Giri's rebellion. He believed in the cause — until Roudra ordered a strike on a civilian supply outpost. Zorvan refused the order. Roudra branded him a deserter. Aurkana disavowed him to avoid political fallout. Now he operates as a freelance missile specialist across the contested zones, selling his Antimatter-class lock-on expertise to whoever pays. One unbreakable rule: no civilian targets, ever.

**Strengths:**
- Lock-on specialist; Antimatter-class missile systems are near-impossible to evade once acquired
- Patient hunter who fights from extreme range with military discipline
- Dual Terrain Mastery from NEO-TERRAn training — effective in any arena

**Weaknesses:**
- Projectile weapons are baseline; he depends entirely on missile lock-on windows
- Disavowed by Aurkana — no resupply network, no backup, no safe harbor
- Deep guilt over the Forge Wars; freezes when missions echo past atrocities

**Rivals:** Roudra (branded him a traitor), Kaevik (rival in the arms trade), Virexa (stole a contract he desperately needed)

---

### 6. KAEVIK — Forge Master of Silkvale

**Rarity:** Legendary | **Role:** Rapid Assault
**Country:** Silkvale | **Culture:** QiNEXIs
**Powers:** Antimatter Class Nuclear P, Dual Terrain Mastery, Normal Thara Resonance

**Backstory:**
Born in Silkvale, the QiNEXi industrial heartland known for pushing weapons technology beyond what anyone thought possible. Kaevik was Silkvale's greatest nuclear weapons engineer — he helped design the Antimatter-class systems that made QiNEXi arms the most sought-after in both the Kingdoms and the Countries. But when he realized Silkvale was covertly supplying Roudra's Asura forces during the Forge Wars — profiting from both sides of the conflict — he took his designs and left. Now he operates an independent weapons lab in contested airspace, selling rapid-fire Nuclear-P systems to anyone who pays. Aaryaveer suspects Kaevik armed Roudra's original coup. Nysera from Inkara broke into his lab and stole prototype schematics. Kaevik fights in the Garuda Trials to prove his weapons are the finest ever built.

**Strengths:**
- Overwhelming rate of fire; QiNEXi rapid-cycle guns never stop shooting
- Self-designed auto-repair systems keep his ship operational mid-combat
- Antimatter-class tech — always one generation ahead of everyone else

**Weaknesses:**
- Missile systems still in prototype; unreliable under sustained pressure
- Trusted by no one — arms dealer reputation makes him a target for every faction
- Overconfidence in his own technology; when it fails, there's no backup plan

**Rivals:** Roudra (former client turned enemy), Nysera (stole his schematics), Aaryaveer (suspects him of fueling the betrayal), Zorvan (arms trade rivalry)

---

### 7. NYSERA — Ghost of Inkara

**Rarity:** Common | **Role:** Long-Range
**Country:** Inkara | **Culture:** INTARIs (South Asian inspired)
**Powers:** Fusion Class Nuclear P, Multi Terrain Mastery, Advance Thara Resonance

**Backstory:**
Raised in Inkara's INTARi border settlements — a region caught between the old Kingdoms and the new Countries, where information is worth more than weapons. Nysera learned to survive by stealing data and selling it before anyone realized it was gone. She sold Ratna Shila's Yaksha defensive positions to rival kingdoms. She infiltrated Kaevik's QiNEXi weapons lab and walked out with Antimatter-class schematics. Her cybernetic eye — a salvaged targeting module reverse-engineered from ancient Vimana tech — lets her lock targets at distances others can barely detect. She doesn't fight up close. She strikes from range and vanishes. She partnered with Virexa once, running intelligence operations together. A job in Elgorn Fall went wrong — Nysera took the data and left Virexa behind. It ended their partnership permanently.

**Strengths:**
- Extreme engagement range; Fusion-class systems tuned for distance strikes
- Advance Thara Resonance — deep energy reserves for sustained long-range pressure
- Information warfare; she maps enemy weaknesses before the fight starts

**Weaknesses:**
- Fragile in close quarters; if someone closes the gap, she's finished
- Cybernetic implants cause chronic pain and need constant maintenance
- No genuine loyalties; allies are temporary, which means support is too

**Rivals:** Kaevik (stole his designs), Ishvaya (sold Ratna Shila's secrets), Virexa (former partner — bitter separation)

---

### 8. VIREXA — Rogue of Elgorn Fall

**Rarity:** Common | **Role:** Glass Cannon
**Country:** Elgorn Fall | **Culture:** ERGOVANs
**Powers:** Fusion Class Nuclear P, Multi Terrain Mastery, Advance Thara Resonance

**Backstory:**
Virexa emerged from Elgorn Fall three years ago — a remote ERGOVAn border region known for producing Fusion-class reactor pilots and nothing else. No family records, no military service file, no academy credentials. She showed up in the underground racing circuits with a stripped-down ship running an overclocked Fusion reactor that hits far above its class. She partnered with Nysera in Inkara for a year, running data-theft operations across the border zones. Then a job went catastrophically wrong — Nysera took the payload and disappeared, leaving Virexa to face capture alone. She spent six months in a detention facility before escaping. Now she flies solo. Her overclocked Fusion reactor amplifies her weapons far beyond what the frame can safely handle. The ship could tear itself apart at any moment. She doesn't care.

**Strengths:**
- Raw damage output that rivals Legendary-class pilots
- Advance Thara Resonance — Fusion reactor gives her stamina to sustain aggressive pushes
- Completely unpredictable; no pattern for opponents to read

**Weaknesses:**
- Overclocked reactor means her ship can't take sustained punishment
- Reckless overcommitment turns winning positions into mutual destruction
- Total isolation — no allies, no faction support, no margin for error

**Rivals:** Nysera (former partner who betrayed her), Zorvan (stole a contract from him), Vyanika (crossed paths in underground circuits)
